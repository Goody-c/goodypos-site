🏷️ Layaway / Installment

Turn "I'll Come Back"
Into a Guaranteed Sale.

Layaway means a customer pays for something little by little over time. They can't afford the full price today, but they want to reserve the item. They pay a deposit now, come back and add more later, and when the full amount is paid the item is theirs. GoodyPOS tracks every payment automatically and sends a WhatsApp receipt each time they pay.

  • ✓Deposit + multiple payment top-ups
  • ✓Visual progress bar per layaway plan
  • ✓Convert to full sale when balance is cleared
  • ✓WhatsApp receipt sent on every payment
